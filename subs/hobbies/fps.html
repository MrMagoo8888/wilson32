FPS & Competitive Games

First Person Shooters have always been a major part of my gaming life. Here are the main ones I play:

Team Fortress 2

A timeless classic. I've logged many hours in TF2. The class-based mechanics and art style are unmatched.

Counter-Strike 2

When I want a more tactical and competitive environment, I hop onto CS2. It requires precision and teamwork that I really enjoy.

Pavlov

Pavlov brings the intensity of shooters like CS into Virtual Reality. It's one of my favorite VR experiences.
